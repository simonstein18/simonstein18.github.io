The Department of Housing and Urban Development sued Meta for violating the Fair Housing Act through their housing advertising algorithm that discriminated against Facebook users based on their race, religion, and national origin. This is the first time that the Department of Housing is challenging algorithmic bias under the Fair Housing Act.

The lawsuit by the Department of Housing against Meta for violating the discriminatory advertising algorithm highlights the need for government intervention and regulation in addressing algorithmic bias and discrimination by big companies. It highlights the importance of diversity and inclusion in algorithm development and testing with diverse and representative people. While the lawsuit is a positive step, it is only the beginning, and the government needs to continue to monitor algorithmic discrimination in other sectors.
